SECTORS

Industries We Serve

Our systems are installed in industrial plants, energy facilities and infrastructure projects across Europe, the Middle East, GCC and beyond.

Manufacturing Industry

Reactive power penalties, VFD distortion and mixed linear/non-linear loads are common challenges. We supply automatic PFC banks and harmonic mitigation systems sized for production environments.

Renewable Energy

Inverter-based systems require specific filtering and transformer solutions. We supply grid interface transformers, power electronics capacitors and reactive compensation for solar and wind installations.

Heavy Industry

Steel plants, foundries and chemical facilities run high-power induction heating and variable speed drives. We supply induction heating capacitors, active harmonic filters and drive protection components.

Utilities & Grid

Automatic compensation at LV and MV level, designed for network stability and distribution power quality. We supply complete switchboards for utility-grade installations.

Oil & Gas

Long motor cables, harsh environments and variable speed drives create specific protection needs. We supply dv/dt filters, sinewave filters and motor protection components for upstream and downstream facilities.

Water Treatment

Pumping stations and treatment plants require reliable, low-maintenance reactive power compensation. We supply automatic PFC banks and motor protection components suited to continuous operation.
