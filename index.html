SECTORS

Industries We Serve

Our solutions are used in industrial plants, infrastructure and energy systems where reactive power compensation, harmonic mitigation and reliable operation are critical.

Manufacturing Industry

Industrial plants typically operate with mixed linear and non-linear loads, including VFDs and automation systems. We supply automatic power factor correction systems and harmonic mitigation solutions to reduce penalties, stabilize voltage and improve overall power quality.

Renewable Energy

Inverter-based systems introduce harmonics and require stable grid interface solutions. We supply transformers, power electronics capacitors and compensation systems for solar and wind installations, supporting reliable integration into the electrical network.

Heavy Industry

Steel plants, foundries and process industries operate high-power equipment such as induction furnaces and large drives. We supply induction heating capacitors, harmonic filters and protection components designed for high current and demanding operating conditions.

Utilities & Grid

Power distribution systems require stable voltage and controlled reactive power at LV and MV levels. We supply capacitor banks, reactors and complete compensation systems for utility and network applications.

Oil & Gas

Facilities in oil and gas environments use variable speed drives, long motor cables and operate under harsh conditions. We supply dv/dt filters, sinewave filters and protection components to improve reliability and extend equipment lifetime.

Water Treatment

Water and wastewater plants rely on continuous operation of pumps and motors. We supply automatic compensation systems and motor protection components to ensure stable operation and reduced maintenance.
