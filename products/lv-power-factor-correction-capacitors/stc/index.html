WHEN TO USE

When to Use STC Capacitors

STC capacitors are designed for standard low-voltage installations where harmonic distortion is low and network conditions are clean.

  • Standard low-voltage installations with no significant non-linear loads
  • Networks with conventional loads such as motors, lighting and heating
  • Automatic and fixed capacitor banks in clean networks
  • Commercial and light industrial applications with low harmonic distortion

PART NUMBER

Part Number Structure

The final suffix *** is completed according to the capacitor dimensions and terminal configuration. Standard and custom configurations are available. Contact our team to confirm the correct part number for your application.

3PF[kVar][Freq][Voltage]STC[***]

Example: 3PF10A400STC*** — 10 kVar, 50 Hz (A), 400 V, STC series. Suffix *** defines dimensions and terminals.

SELECTION LIMITS

When a More Robust Series May Be Required

If any of the following conditions apply to your installation, a more robust series such as STC HD, INC or INB HD should be considered.

  • Installations with variable speed drives (VFDs) or frequency converters
  • UPS systems, rectifiers or other non-linear loads
  • High switching frequency duty in automatic capacitor banks
  • Measured THDi above 5% or THDu above 3%
  • NLL greater than 10%
  • Demanding industrial conditions with elevated thermal or electrical stress
