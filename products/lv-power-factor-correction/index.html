LV REACTIVE POWER COMPENSATION

Why LV Power Factor Correction Matters

Low-voltage power factor correction systems compensate reactive power and improve efficiency in industrial installations. In networks with variable loads, automatic systems maintain stable power factor. In systems with harmonic distortion — driven by VFDs, UPS or non-linear loads — detuned solutions are required to prevent resonance and protect electrical equipment.

Gruppo Energia manufactures LV power factor correction solutions in Italy and supplies panel builders, EPC contractors and industrial distributors across Europe, the Middle East, GCC and Asia.
