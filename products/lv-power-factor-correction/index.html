LV Power Factor Correction Solutions

Solutions designed to improve power factor, reduce reactive energy costs and support stable operation in low-voltage industrial systems, including installations with variable loads and harmonic distortion.

LOW-VOLTAGE REACTIVE POWER COMPENSATION

Low-Voltage Reactive Power Compensation

Low-voltage power factor correction systems are used to compensate reactive power and improve efficiency in industrial installations. In networks with variable loads, automatic systems maintain stable power factor, while in systems with harmonic distortion, detuned solutions are required to prevent resonance and protect electrical equipment.

Gruppo Energia manufactures LV power factor correction solutions in Italy and supplies panel builders, EPC contractors and industrial distributors across Europe, the Middle East, GCC and Asia.
