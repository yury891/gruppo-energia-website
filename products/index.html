Power Quality, Power Factor Correction and Industrial Electrical Components

A complete range of solutions for reactive power compensation, harmonic mitigation, transformer applications and drive protection in industrial power systems.

INDUSTRIAL PRODUCT RANGE

Our product portfolio covers low-voltage and medium-voltage power factor correction systems, harmonic filtering solutions, transformers and drive protection components. These solutions are used in industrial plants, infrastructure and energy systems where stable operation, reduced losses and compliance with power quality requirements are essential.

Gruppo Energia manufactures industrial electrical components in Italy and supplies panel builders, EPC contractors and industrial distributors across Europe, the Middle East, GCC and Asia.
