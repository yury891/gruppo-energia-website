CUSTOM MANUFACTURING

Technical Solutions Beyond the Standard Product Range

Some applications require more than a standard catalogue product. Gruppo Energia supports customers who need custom capacitor solutions, unfinished components or special configurations developed according to electrical, mechanical or integration requirements.

This includes capacitor elements prepared to customer specification, unfinished or sprayed components, custom electrical configurations and solutions developed for OEM and industrial applications.

Gruppo Energia develops and manufactures these solutions in Italy and supports customers across Europe, the Middle East, GCC and Asia.

CUSTOM CAPABILITIES

What We Can Supply

Custom Capacitor Configurations

Capacitor solutions developed according to required capacitance, voltage, dimensions or application constraints.

Unfinished Capacitor Elements

Semi-finished capacitor elements for customers who complete integration or final assembly internally.

Sprayed Capacitor Elements

Capacitor elements prepared and sprayed according to customer specifications and production requirements.

Custom Terminal Design

Special terminal configurations developed for specific electrical layouts, mounting constraints or direct connection requirements.

OEM-Oriented Capacitor Components

Custom components supplied for OEM systems, inverter assemblies, power electronics and special industrial equipment.

Project-Specific Development

Technical support for applications requiring non-standard electrical or mechanical configuration.
